Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, GTA Simulator is completely free to play. There are no hidden fees or in-app purchases required to enjoy the full game experience.

No downloads are required. GTA Simulator runs directly in your web browser on both desktop and mobile devices.

Your game progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data may result in loss of progress.
